Appendix

Affliction:

Poisons

No other affliction is so prevalent as poison. From the fangs of a viper to the ichor-stained assassins blade, poison is a constent threat. Poisons can be cured by successful saving throws and spells such as neutralize poison.

Contact poisons are contracted the moment someone touches the poison with his bare skin. Such poisons can be used as injury poisons.

The following samples present just some of the possibilities when creating poisons.
